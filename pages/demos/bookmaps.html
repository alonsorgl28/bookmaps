Bookmaps

A real-time treemap of Revoluciones tecnologicas y capital financiero. Every block is a chapter or threshold section. Move your cursor: blocks under your attention expand, revealing more conceptual detail. Distant blocks compress. The entire layout recomputes every frame.

Why Pretext

Each block calls layout() every frame to determine how much text fits at its current dimensions. With DOM measurement, this would force repeated reflow. Pretext makes it cheap enough that the whole map can stay alive under the cursor.

What You're Seeing

  • 19 blocks covering the front matter, the three main parts, and the epilogue.
  • Cursor-driven expansion. Nearby chapters grow, revealing more of the book's structure and argument.
  • Category filtering. Hover or tap the legend bar to isolate one part of the book.
  • Live performance counter. Layout calls and time measured with performance.now().

Why This Book Fits The Form

The book is already a map of phases, agents, tensions and turning points. A treemap makes that systemic logic visible before you ever read linearly.
